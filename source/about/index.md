I am **Horace Chan**, a mechanical engineering undergraduate student interested in the intersection of mechanical systems, intelligent agents, and AI-assisted research workflows.

我希望这个网站成为一个长期更新的个人主页和学习记录空间：既展示项目，也沉淀我在机械工程、机器人、机器学习和具身智能方向上的学习过程。

## Background

My academic background is in **Mechanical Engineering**. I am building a foundation in mechanical design, robot systems, machine learning, deep learning, and robot control, while gradually connecting these areas through practical projects and reading notes.

I am especially interested in how engineering intuition from physical systems can support the study of embodied AI and robotic control.

## Engineering Skills

- Mechanical structure design with mainstream 3D CAD tools, including SolidWorks
- Programming with Python, C, and C++
- Learning and applying machine learning and deep learning fundamentals
- Building structured study systems and AI-assisted workflows for technical learning
- Reading and organizing papers, project notes, and implementation records

## Research Interests

My current interests include:

- **AI-assisted workflows** for learning, research, note-taking, and engineering productivity
- **Vision-Language-Action Models (VLA)** and their role in robot learning
- **Embodied AI**, especially the connection between perception, language, action, and physical interaction
- **Robotics and robot control**, including modeling, control strategies, and learning-based methods
- Mechanical design for intelligent robotic systems

## Long-Term Goal

My long-term goal is to understand how intelligent agents and robot systems are constructed, from mechanical design and control to learning-based decision-making. I hope to participate in research and engineering practice related to embodied intelligence, robot control, and AI-assisted scientific workflows.

This website will document that process in public: notes, projects, experiments, reading paths, and reflections as they become concrete.
